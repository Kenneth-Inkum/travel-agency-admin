Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account information (name, email, phone)
  • Business details and travel agency information
  • Booking and reservation data
  • Customer information for travel arrangements
  • Usage data and analytics

2. How We Use Information

Your information helps us:

  • Process and manage travel bookings
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Generate business insights and analytics
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Secure data storage and backup systems
  • Access controls and authentication

4. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and view your personal data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
